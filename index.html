Automatic Grading for Short Answer Essay

The system automatically grades student short answer essays by matching the wording of student responses against reference answers according to ROUGE evaluation metric. ROUGE (Recall-Oriented Understudy for Gisting Evaluation) measures the quality of summaries by looking at overlapping units such as n-grams, word sequences or pairs. Through the evaluation of the precision, recall and F1 scoreof these overlaps, it provides an objectiveized but reasonable qualification criteria to distinguish student performance with fairness.
